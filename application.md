<h2>2008</h2>

<p>My proposal for an NSA Young Investigator Grant from 2008 (which was funded):<br><strong>Caveat lector:</strong> At the time NSA had much less strict criteria for proposal structure than the NSF (no page limit, less specific review criteria, a normal CV instead of the NSF bio), so do not model an NSF proposal directly on these documents. It seems that they now have different rules, still not the same as the NSF. You have been warned.</p>
